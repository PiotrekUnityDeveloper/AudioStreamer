Main Features


  • Create an Account and use it on multiple devices
  • Play music online or download it for later
  • +1000 Songs and Soundtracks
  • Most songs come with bundled instrumental
  • Karaoke mode for a night party :D
  • Public Playlists and a personal Queue
  • Play Multiple channels at once. Mute drums or other instruments!
  • Support for popular audio extensions (*.mp3, *.wav, *.ogg ...)
  • Support for content folders and .streamer files
  • Real-time editable Lyrics with a bunch of features
  • Create Playlists and Groups
  • Built-in Audio Converter, Trimmer and Equalizer!
  • No fucking ads!
  • 100% FREE and OPEN SOURCE Project. You dont risk a virus!
  • Upload your music completely free!
